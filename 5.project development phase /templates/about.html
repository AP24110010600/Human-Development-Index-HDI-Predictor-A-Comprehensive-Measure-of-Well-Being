About Human Development Index Predictor

The Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistical composite index used to measure a country's overall development. It considers three major indicators:

  • Life Expectancy
  • Education
  • Gross National Income (GNI) Per Capita

This application predicts the HDI score using a Machine Learning model trained on historical HDI data.

Development Categories

HDI Score Category
0.800 - 1.000 Very High Human Development
0.700 - 0.799 High Human Development
0.550 - 0.699 Medium Human Development
Below 0.550 Low Human Development
